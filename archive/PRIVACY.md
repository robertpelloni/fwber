# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 12, 2025

## Introduction

fwber ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our proximity-based social networking service.

**By using fwber, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Date of birth (for age verification)
- Gender identity
- Display name/username

### 2. Profile Information

You may choose to provide:
- Avatar/profile pictures (AI-generated in Avatar Mode)
- Bio/description
- Interests and preferences
- Relationship preferences
- Personal attributes for matching

### 3. Location Data

We collect precise geolocation data when you:
- Use the Local Pulse feature
- Browse proximity artifacts
- Enable location-based matching

**Location Privacy:**
- Your exact location is **never** disclosed to other users
- We apply fuzzing algorithms to obscure precise coordinates
- Location data is used only for proximity calculations and matching
- You can disable location services at any time (this will limit proximity features)

### 4. Content You Create

We store content you generate:
- Proximity artifacts (chat invitations, board posts, announcements)
- Messages in proximity chatrooms
- Flags/reports you submit
- Reactions and interactions

### 5. Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:
- Device information (type, OS, browser)
- IP address
- Usage patterns and activity logs
- API request metadata
- Session information
- Crash reports and error logs

### 6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:
- Authentication and session management
- Preferences and settings
- Analytics and performance monitoring
- Security and fraud prevention

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## How We Use Your Information

### 1. Core Service Delivery

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Provide proximity-based matching and discovery
- Display nearby proximity artifacts
- Calculate compatibility scores
- Enable communication features
- Enforce Avatar Mode policies (if enabled)

### 2. Safety and Security

We use your information to:
- Detect and prevent spam, abuse, and fraud
- Enforce Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
- Implement shadow throttling for policy violators
- Detect geolocation spoofing attempts
- Respond to flags and moderation reports
- Protect against security threats

### 3. Service Improvement

We use aggregated and anonymized data to:
- Analyze usage patterns
- Improve matching algorithms
- Optimize proximity calculations
- Enhance user experience
- Develop new features

### 4. Communications

We may contact you for:
- Service announcements
- Account security notifications
- Policy updates
- Feature updates (with your consent)

You can opt out of non-essential communications in your account settings.

## Data Retention

### Active Accounts
- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Profile information: Retained until you delete it
- Messages: Retained according to expiry settings
- Proximity artifacts: Automatically expire after TTL (Time-To-Live)

### Deleted Accounts
- Account deletion is permanent and irreversible
- Most data is deleted within 30 days
- Some data may be retained for legal or safety purposes:
  - Fraud prevention records
  - Legal compliance data
  - Aggregated analytics (anonymized)

### Content Expiry
- Proximity artifacts expire automatically (configurable TTL)
- Expired content is permanently deleted
- Chatroom messages may expire based on settings

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only in these circumstances:

1. **With Other Users (Limited)**

2. **Service Providers**
   - Hosting and infrastructure providers
   - Analytics services (anonymized data)
   - Email delivery services
   - Payment processors (if applicable)

3. **Legal Requirements**
   - To comply with legal obligations
   - To protect our rights and safety
   - To prevent fraud or illegal activity
   - In response to valid legal requests

4. **Business Transfers**
   - In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale
   - Users will be notified of any ownership changes

## Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Authentication:** Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access restrictions
- **Monitoring:** Real-time security monitoring and alerts
- **Auditing:** Regular security audits and penetration testing
- **Backups:** Encrypted backups with access controls

**However, no system is 100% secure. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication when available.**

## Children's Privacy

fwber is **not intended for users under 18 years of age.**

We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will immediately delete their account and data.

If you believe a minor has created an account, please report it to [safety@fwber.example.com].
