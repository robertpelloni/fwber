


Features FetLife appears to have that fwber does not fully have yet:

 1. Structured interest taxonomy on profiles — profile-level lists for roles, orientations, and interests/fetishes
with strong identity/discovery value.
 2. Explicit relationship links between members — public profile relationship graph/statuses beyond matching.
 3. User blogs / writings / journals — long-form posting with comments, separate from feed posts.
 4. Topic-centric community groups/forums — deeper forum-style discussion spaces, not just chatrooms or lightweight
groups.
 5. Interest pages / tag ecosystems — browsable hubs for specific interests with attached discussion and discovery.
 6. Granular audience controls for content — e.g. public vs friends-only for posts/media/writings at a richer level.
 7. Community-first profile discovery — less dating-centric, more identity/community/subculture browsing.
 8. Mature event-community linkage — tighter connection between groups, local scenes, and event participation.
 9. Persistent social graph features — friend/follow/community reputation patterns stronger than fwber’s current
matching-first model.
 10. Richer discussion/comment culture around user-generated content — comments on writings/media/posts as
first-class social objects.
 11. Dedicated kink/interest compatibility layer — fwber has matching/AI, but not the same explicit structured
interest overlap model.
 12. Stronger creator/community publishing surface — recurring posters, essays, discussions, local organizers, niche
 communities.

What fwber already overlaps with: events, groups, messaging, profiles, feeds, media, local discovery, and some
moderation/community features. The biggest true gaps are the structured identity taxonomy, writing/forum depth,
relationship graph, and interest-centered community discovery.

Best candidate ideas to borrow without cloning FetLife:

 - Structured interest graph for profiles and matching
 - Long-form journals / field notes
 - Topic hubs tied to Local Pulse + groups
 - Relationship/status links
 - Friends-only / circle-only visibility controls
 - Scene-based local discovery around interests, not just proximity

If you want, I’d rank these by highest product value for fwber rather than just “most similar to FetLife.”
